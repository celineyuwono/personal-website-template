About The Owner

Celine Yuwono is a Senior in UNC-Chapel Hill majoring in Information Science. She named the computer "Apple Juice" and created the Apple Farm website.

Celine is currently doing a field experience in Computer Services. In Computer Services, students do things such as: Rebooting computers, checking inventories, and assembling PC parts. When there is nothing to do in Computer Services, Celine does html or label stuff.

During school breaks, Celine likes to travel and visit nature. She likes to go to places such as cities, beaches, mountains, canyons and fields. This picture is taken in a Lavender Field South of London.
